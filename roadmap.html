Softmap’s YouTube Roadmap

December 2024–January 2025 ▾

Theme: The Foundation Era

Goals:

  • Create a channel identity
  • Upload at least 4 full videos + 4 Shorts
  • Lock in a weekly posting rhythm

Sub Goals:

  • Reach 20 subscribers
  • Upload minimum 4 videos
  • Gain at least 10 comments total

Video Ideas:

  • I Made 7 Videos on a School iPad (Here’s What Happened)
  • Installing Windows 11 on Trash Hardware (and it kinda works)
  • How I Built a Custom Keyboard for My Teacher
  • Short: Here’s how I make videos on a school iPad

Tasks:

  • Update banner, about section, playlists
  • Set up Notion or Google Sheet for idea tracking
  • Start commenting on tech/creator channels
  • Record your workspace upgrade journey (even if small)

February–March 2025 ▾

Theme: Experiment & Iterate

Goals:

  • Upload 6 full videos
  • Make 6 YouTube Shorts
  • Try at least 2 new content types (vlog, tutorial, coding log)

Sub Goals:

  • Reach 50 subscribers
  • Upload minimum 6 videos
  • Increase average view duration to 40%+

Video Ideas:

  • How I Use My iPad for School + YouTube
  • I Tried Using Raspberry Pi as My Main Computer for a Week
  • My ADHD vs. Making an App from Scratch
  • This App Actually Helped Me Focus in School (No Cap)

Tasks:

  • Use Canva or Photopea to improve thumbnails
  • Learn basic video analytics (CTR, retention)
  • Do 1 video reacting to an older one

April–May 2025 ▾

Theme: Mini Glow-Up

Goals:

  • Improve production: lighting, sound, or editing workflow
  • Release a small “tech documentary” or mini challenge series
  • Hit 50–75 subs by end of May

Sub Goals:

  • Reach 75 subscribers
  • Upload minimum 8 videos
  • Gain 20+ comments per video

Video Ideas:

  • I Used Only School Tech for a Week (Pain)
  • Windows 8 vs 11 — Which One Deserves to Be Erased?
  • This Tech Hack Changed My School Life
  • Building My Dream Setup (on a 14-year-old’s budget)

Tasks:

  • Ask for basic gear for holidays (clip-on mic, tripod)
  • Test Shorts hooks: “This broke my laptop,” “I regret this install”
  • Start ending each video with a CTA: “Tell me what to try next!”

June–July 2025 ▾

Theme: Momentum Mode

Goals:

  • Post 8+ full videos
  • Reach 100 subscribers
  • Launch a regular “series” (weekly builds, tech tests, or ADHD hacks)

Sub Goals:

  • Reach 100 subscribers
  • Upload 8+ videos
  • Gain consistent comments and engagement

Video Ideas:

  • Why Your School Laptop Is a Secret Weapon (Kinda)
  • I Tried Coding with ADHD — Here’s the Chaos
  • How to Actually Fix Windows Being Slow (Kid Edition)
  • Series: Fixing Tech My School Gave Up On

Tasks:

  • Create a community tab post (once you unlock it)
  • Try collabing with a microcreator (even just shoutouts)
  • Say “Subscribe for more [X]” in the first 20 seconds of videos

August–September 2025 ▾

Theme: Brand Expansion

Goals:

  • Hit 150–250 subscribers
  • Add in more searchable, trending topics
  • Make videos that feel like “Softmap” — style, humor, energy

Sub Goals:

  • Reach 250 subscribers
  • Upload minimum 10 videos
  • Increase engagement and sharing

Video Ideas:

  • Why I Still Use Windows 8 in 2026
  • I Tried Running VisionOS on a School iPad (kinda)
  • This Browser Changed My Focus Game
  • Make a Parallax Game in Scratch (2026 Edition)

Tasks:

  • Update branding — banner, intro, maybe emojis
  • Build a channel trailer
  • Create a public idea submission form

October–November 2025 ▾

Theme: Level-Up & Monetize

Goals:

  • Reach 300–500 subscribers
  • Have 50 total videos uploaded
  • Start planning monetization paths

Sub Goals:

  • Reach 500 subscribers
  • Upload 12+ videos
  • Establish a regular posting schedule

Video Ideas:

  • Making Money as a 14-Year-Old YouTuber (No Scams)
  • Best Free Tech Tools for Teen Creators
  • How I Made My First 100 Videos with $0 Budget
  • What I Wish I Knew Before Starting My Tech Channel

Tasks:

  • Set up Ko-fi or Buy Me a Coffee
  • Consider affiliate links (Amazon, gear, software)
  • Create a business email for collabs

December 2025: 1-Year Anniversary Special ▾

Theme: The Creator Evolution

Goals:

  • Hit 500+ subscribers
  • Have 50+ videos uploaded
  • Be known for consistent, creative tech content with humor and student relatability

Video Ideas:

  • 1 Year of YouTube at 14 — What I Learned, Regret, and Loved
  • Reacting to My First Video (Filmed on a School iPad)
  • Rebuilding My Old Projects with My New Skills

Tasks:

  • Plan next year’s roadmap
  • Engage with community for feedback
  • Celebrate your creator journey publicly
